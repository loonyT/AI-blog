Adopt the new trend:

A recent study confirms that Chat is the preferred channel among millennials

Posted by Bastien Deresteau on the 7th of March 2021

The researchers conducted the study from the 1st of January 2017 till the 31st of December 2017. They collected data from 40 149 88 chat messages. From 13 different industries and diverse companies. .

The scientists chose the business who were using live chat for: Customer service and support, or online sales.

After, the experts filtered the data and only kept:

  • Customers with established live chat service
  • Customers with 30 chats or more per month

This research is thus to improve your live chat strategy. Which is a required step. If you want to raise your customer satisfaction rate and your profitability. Indeed, this study showed that you can get a higher ROI if you invest in a virtual chat system..

The rise in ROI happened because more and more customers are choosing live chat. As their preferred channel of communication. For instance, scientists noticed a 180% increase in average chats per month.

  • It allows them to multitask.
  • It saves time, resources, and attention.
  • It raises the speed of interaction and increases resolution time.
  • It allows resolved interactions

Ensuring a successful live chat system

In the end, to ensure a successful live chat system: You must create a customer service strategy promoting it. And, since 43.78% of customers access support from a mobile device , you also have to make it responsive.

Hence, to enhance the customer experience and improve the satisfaction rate: You can integrate a virtual assistant into your live chat system.
