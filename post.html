Want to improve your service desk productivity ?

Check the article !

Posted by Bastien Deresteau on July 21, 2020

Often, in large companies, you must send a mail or create a ticket to contact your service desk. In other words, that means you do not have a direct link with the support team. This can lead to time loss and frustration.

You can ease that process by improving the productivity and the quality of your IT service desk. For instance, by using a virtual assistant , your agents will be able to handle many chats in parallel and use predefined messages to save time..

Furthermore, you can integrate a Chatbot with your ITSM platform through popular chat apps such as:

  • Slack
  • Teams
  • Skype for business

Imagine if you and your teams could “talk” to a super-agent that handles, centralizes, and automates all your IT problems.

Machine learning and chatbots

Usually, that kind of digital assistant takes the form of a Chatbot which uses Machine Learning to answer the most common requests. That mechanism keeps your agents focused on important tasks. If the virtual assistant cannot help, it will assign the chat to a live agent, based on skills and availability.

You can implement the solution with a Workflow engine that features entirely configurable interactions with end users, to address every requests your employees might have.

Support outside of office hours

Additionally, the technology provides support outside of office hours. It can, for instance, and when required: transfer chats that it receives during the night to the support agent. Moreover, it can create tickets in the ITSM system, automatically and according to the answers given by the user input. Thus, you have support all the time, even when no human agent is available.

Chatbots are the futur of ITSM !

Consequently, thanks to a top tier artificial intelligence tool, combined with human interaction, you can deliver high-quality support at a lower cost, while reducing the workload on your support agents. As a result, to have fewer IT and communication problems, with more time for innovation and collaboration, you can explore the chatbot solutions.
