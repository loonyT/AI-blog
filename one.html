How to improve your Business performance:

Top 10 reasons why you need to adopt a Chatbot

Posted by Bastien Deresteau on the 7th of March 2021

First, what kind of conversational agent does your business need?

There are three main types of Chatbot you can integrate into your ITSM:

  • Rule based one :

You interact with them through predefined options and menus. Though the choices are limited and it is not the fastest type of Chatbot. When it comes to answering customer requests.

  • Machine learning based ones :

Those types of virtual assistants use machine learning to process the user’s inputs. And, formulate an answer based on the analysis of specific keywords and phrases.

  • Artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing based ones:

That type is a combination of the two types mentioned above. It can analyze the conversation’s context and the user’s behavior. Then, remember the content to improve its answers over time, in an autonomous manner.

Four steps to determine which would be the best for your business:


  1. You have to know what the purpose of your Chatbot is. Does it answer simple or complex questions?
  2. Determine who controls the interaction: is it yourself or the end-user? Does your customer ask the question without restrictions? Or does he pick from predefined menus?
  3. Find on which communication channels you will use the virtual agent. After, ask yourself about the integration requirements.
  4. Time and money considerations: An AI Chatbot with uses NLP and ML costs more than a rule-based one.

Hence, for its customer service or support, a business that uses a Chatbot can benefit from the following advantages:


  • Achieve more with less:

    AI conversation agents deflect trivial ticket being sent to human agents

  • Increase your website conversion rate:

    Live chat interaction for support is an efficient way to retain your leads and engage with new customers.

  • Tailor-made opportunity:

    The possibility to configure bot messages and to personalize the behavior of the virtual Agent.

  • Improve your ITSM productivity

  • Streamline your employee onboarding/offboarding process :

    By automatically allocating the proper resources with the Chatbot. Likewise, you can speed up the approval process if you automate the workflow with a virtual agent. Which will then notify all the relevant approvers

  • Enhance your knowledge base management:

    A conversational agent can help a user when it suggests the relevant article/information or for real-time feedback. It can also make analysis of requests and interactions to show what is missing in the database.

  • Reduced business risks:

    Chabot’s solution allows you to predict an incident and report a ticket. You can thus save money by getting rid of delays and large service disruption. Moreover, your agents will be informed about any possible SLA infringement.

  • Leverage customer behavior data:

    To hand over a more personalized and significant experience.

  • Enable and improve Omnichannel communications:

    A conversational agent can be integrated to Microsoft Teams, Skype for Business, Slack, WhatsApp, WeChat, Telegram, Signal, Facebook Messenger, Viber, etc.

  • Scale your support ability to the next level:

    A human agent can only handle a few conversations at the same time. A Chatbot can handle thousands of them without mistakes and breaks with a 24*7 availability.
