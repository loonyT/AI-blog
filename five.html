4 AI techs that will impact your Service Desk

Check these four techs below !

Posted by Bastien Deresteau on the seventh of MArch 2021

Since a few years, artificial intelligence is more and more popular. Especially in the discussions of IT managers. On the other hand, companies still try to find when and how to invest. Thus, each business set up its own IT strategy for its Service Desk.

One of the biggest challenges companies face is the increasing number of tickets. Another challenge is to raise the productivity of support without a new workforce. All this to handle the requests of the end-user passing by an increasing number of channels of communication.

How can AI help you overcome these challenges?

  1. Chatbot and virtual assistant

    2. Chat communication is now common among companies. Especially to contact the Service Desk. However, human intervention is still required.

    A virtual assistant can filter the requests. To allow your human agents to focus on the issues that require human intervention.

    To provide your clients with automated human-like answers, you can use a Chatbot. These kind of request are a good example:

    • Access to the FAQ
    • Basic support request
    • Password reset

    You can then calculate your ROI according to these indicators:

    • Performance of Self Desk tools based on chat interactions
    • The number of IT requests for the first degree of support
    • More human resources available and thus more time for projects with a real added value

  2. Natural language processing

    3. It is a technology that focuses on language interactions between humans and computers. It helps AI interfaces to better understand and contextualize human language. To improve the quality and the precision of the Chatbot interactions, for instance.

    If AI techs can understand what the end-user asks, in his mother tongue, the adoption rate will skyrocket!

  3. Context

    4. To provide the end-user with the relevant and efficient user experience, a virtual assistant has to understand the context in which the request is made:

    • Position of the agent within the company
    • Credentials of the agent for system access
    • Who is the manager in charge
    • What are the devices the agent use

  4. Automation of cross plate form actions

    5. If you allow your end-user to automatically interact with your CRM and other companiesâ€™ plate form, through a virtual assistant. You will have a real added value and a major gain of productivity. Indeed, the user wants a quick answer and often, does not want to look by himself into heavy documentation.

Conclusion

The constant engagement with your workforce and clients, through AI, does not start nor end with a Chatbot. There are a lot of other factors you have to take into account. Such as:

  • Fast gains with measurable ROI
  • Contextualized interactions
  • Automated access to the knowledge base/FAQ

Furthermore, make sure that you can issue reports about end-users interactions. So that you can implement continuous improvement.

Consider all those factors properly and your agents and customers will adopt automation faster! This will make more time for your teams to create added value for your business.
